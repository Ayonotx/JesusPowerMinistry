Apostle Dr. Jones Boateng Library was commissioned on the 24th April, 2024, it was named after Apostle Bismark Akomeah's close friend who had been a backbone and huge support to this project.
The Library has in stock over 3000 christian literatures, books from both local and international authors, dictionaries,  encyclopedias, bible commentaries, bibles, children bibles and story books, learning materials for children to serve not only out clients but the community as well. 

The Library launched the Apst. Akomeah's children's reading club, on February 28, 2026 to help children in the community with academic learning by providing extra classes for them whilst nurturing their spiritual growth.
