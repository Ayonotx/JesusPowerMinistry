About Us

Welcome to Jesus Power

A place of spiritual transformation, academic excellence, and divine encounter.

Our Mission

Jesus Power School of Ministry is not just a School. It is a journey of faith, discovery, and transformation. Through gaining a deeper understanding of God's Word and its practical application to everyday life, we raise champions to advance God's Kingdom.

Located in Dawhenya, Abbey City, our center offers a serene environment for spiritual growth, ministerial training, and personal retreats.
