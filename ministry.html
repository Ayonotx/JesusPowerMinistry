Welcome to JPSOM

The Jesus Power School of Ministry (JPSOM) officially opened its doors on 24th April 2024, welcoming its first cohort of students with a mission to raise, equip, and empower believers for impactful Christian ministry.

Founded through the vision God placed in the heart of Apostle Bismark Osei Akomeah, JPSOM was established to provide accessible, practical, and biblically sound ministry training for Christians from all walks of life. Whether you have a strong academic background or none at all, our goal is to help you discover, develop, and fulfill God's calling upon your life.

At JPSOM, we believe that every believer with a passion for serving God deserves quality ministerial training, mentorship, and spiritual development in preparation for effective Kingdom service.

2. Diploma in Ministerial Studies

Offered in partnership with Global University, Berean School of the Bible (Springfield, Missouri, USA)

Library

Apostle Dr. Jones Boateng Library

Commissioned on 24th April 2024 • Librarian: Min. Rebecca Nuamah

The library was named after Apostle Bismark Akomeah's close friend who has been a backbone and huge support to this project. It houses over 3,000 Christian literatures, books from local and international authors, dictionaries, encyclopedias, Bible commentaries, Bibles, children's Bibles, story books, and learning materials for children.

The library also launched the Apostle Akomeah Children's Reading Club on February 28, 2026, to help children in the community with academic learning while nurturing their spiritual growth.
